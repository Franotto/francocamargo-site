Who we are

A small firm with the conviction of a large one.

Founded in 1986, Hale & Wren was built around a simple belief: that exceptional representation is personal. We keep our roster deliberately lean so that every client works directly with a partner who knows their matter intimately.

From the boardroom to the courtroom, we bring the same discipline — meticulous preparation, candid counsel, and an unwillingness to settle for the expedient.
